## Core Capabilities of iZotope RX

### Intelligent Spectral Analysis & Repair
- **Spectral Editing:** Visualize sound in a 3D spectrogram where **iZotope RX** allows you to cut, heal, and manipulate specific frequencies without affecting the rest of the audio.
- **Music Rebalance:** Independently control the gain of vocals, bass, percussion, and other instruments using the AI-powered **iZotope RX** module to create stems or rebalance mixes.
- **Dialogue Isolate:** Separate dialogue from background noise with extreme precision, even in challenging real-world recording environments.
- **De-click & De-crackle:** Automatically detect and remove impulse noises, vinyl crackle, and mouth clicks using adaptive algorithms exclusive to **iZotope RX**.
- **De-noise:** Reduce constant and dynamic background noise (like air conditioning, wind, or electrical hum) while preserving the natural character of the original signal.
